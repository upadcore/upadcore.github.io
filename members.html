The Application Process

Your first step towards the orange path

Your own kind of creative

You are a UP student who aspires to unleash your creative side while maintaining a business front. You are ready to exemplify excellence and commit yourself to an organization which truly understands your needs.

Your application process is as simple as spelling ADCORE. Go through with the following steps and be a part of this creativity.

A

Attendance
and Tambay Week

Attend AdCore’s events. You can attend more than the required number of events. Complete the required 30 hours of Tambay. Hang out at the AdHouse and get to know other applicants and members.

D

Date with Family

A buddy family (composed of 2-3 members and 3-6 applicants) will have to accomplish buddy family tasks, which will have corresponding points. Each family will have to meet the required number of points announced by Team AdDition.

C

Complete the
Sigsheet

Ask the members to sign your sigsheet, especially the ExeCore and Team AdDition. Design it as creatively as you can and enjoy getting to know the members more.

O

Offer Help

Offer help to the AdCore teams and be an intern! This is your chance to have hands-on experience of handling an AdCore project and learning how teams work in AdCore.

R

Revalida

In advertising, the pitching of ideas is what we call a Revalida. This incorporates all the learnings you and your groupmates acquired throughout your application as you present to the ExeCore and Team AdDition.

E

End Interview

This will test your knowledge about AdCore and your dedication to join the organization.

Creatives (CRT)

It is the design powerhouse of the organization that brings to life ideas of the different departments. The Creatives Department is dedicated to delivering quality outputs and assuring that each product of design reflects what the specific project and organization is truly about.

ACLE

Team ACLE is in charge of UP AdCore’s Alternative Classroom Learning Experience. They aim to impart fresh knowledge to the UP student body by inviting professionals from the advertising industry to give students a chance to experience and learn more about the exciting world of advertising.

AdSchool

AdSchool is the official design training vessel of the org. The team organizes learning sessions which ensure that AdCore mems and apps develop a leveled-up design sensibility and become equipped with competitive creative skills to come up with more innovative and quality design works

AdSpeak

Team AdSpeak shall organize an idea generation competition exclusive for AdCore members in partnership with a corporate brand client. AdSpeak will foster members’ skills in marketing and advertising through mentoring sessions and pitching, making UP AdCore members more competitive in the university and in the real world.

BrandCore

BrandCore aims to solidify the AdCore brand and standardize how AdCore is perceived. This is done externally by conceptualizing and creating AdCore’s official portfolio and primer and internally by producing case studies for past projects. Team BrandCore is also in charge of entering AdCore projects in advertising and communication competitions.

Finance and Corporate Affairs (FCA)

As the organization’s left brain, the Finance and Corporate Affairs Department (FCA) ensures effective allocation and use of funds through strategic planning and budget control. This side of the organization also develops and maintains healthy relations with clients, corporate or not, giving members the chance to handle real life projects. Moreover, the FCA department makes use of the organization’s talents to generate money.

Finance

Team Finance handles the financial matters of the organization. Through merchandise sales, equipment rentals, activations, and income-generating events, the team keeps AdCore’s financial status healthy. It also implements rules and budget protocols geared towards better allocation of the organization’s resources.

AdVance

AdVance brings the advertising world closer to AdCore mems and apps by giving them a glimpse of how advertising works and how to be in an advertising career path. The team provides learning opportunities about up-to-date, effective and responsible advertising through the seminars and workshops conducted by reputable and big-time advertising names.

Punch Creatives

Punch Creatives is the corporate affairs arm of the organization. From seeking clients and negotiating deals, to executing creative projects, this team gives its members an opportunity to experience what it is like to work in an advertising agency.

Human Resources (HR)

The Human Resources Department (HR) is dedicated to ensuring that the people who give life to the organization are well-taken care of. They are responsible for providing the members, as well as the applicants, a venue to bond and build long-lasting relationships. They make certain that all necessary updates and information concerning the members are properly disseminated, allowing open communication within the organization. Ultimately, HR aims to strengthen the members’ commitment to the organization.

AdDition

AdDition aims to add members to the organization through pooling quality applicants who shall be given the chance to experience how it is to be part of the core. The team is responsible for creating the application process and monitoring the applicants as they progress during the semester.

AdHesive

AdHesive aims to strengthen the commitment of its members to assure that good and dedicated people will eventually lead future generations of members. This is accomplished through innovative team building and recreational activities, simple get-togethers and proper evaluation of members’ performance.

AdHere

AdHere serves as the bridge between the members and alumni. This team is dedicated to ensuring that the people who became members of the organization are still updated and informed about the activities of the org. They are responsible for providing long-lasting relationships among the alumni and the organization.

Public Relations and Promotions (PRP)

The Public Relations and Promotions Department (PRP) is the voice of the organization in charge of finding innovative ways to make use of promotional channels both inside and outside the university. The department ensures that the organization, its events and its causes are communicated excellently to its target market and that its advertisements stand out from the clutter of others in the industry. PRP also serves as the official liaison to the Office of Student Affairs. From strategic planning for each project to negotiating with other organizations, it’s on PRP.

AdVocate

AdVocate is AdCore’s social responsibility arm which has a specific thrust and cause at its core every semester, achieved by partnering with clients and developing creative PR campaigns that propagate these causes to a wider audience.

Juiceletter

Juiceletter is in charge of the official publication of UP AdCore. From helpful articles about the advertising world to proper documentation of the events during the sem, Juiceletter is there to give members updates not just from inside the org, but from the outside world as well.

UP Career Fair

The UP Career Fair (formerly University Job Fair) is a widely-anticipated event that aims to provide job and internship opportunities to UP students across all colleges. UPCF is organized by the USC, UP JPIA, and of course, UP AdCore -- its sole promotional arm. The UPCF Execution Team strengthens UP AdCore’s image in the university as a student-run advertising agency that delivers excellent output.

UP Fair

UP Fair is a week-long annual music festival with nightly social themes, ranging from the arts to calls for changes in the status quo. It is held at the Sunken Garden and features both established and up-and-coming OPM artists.

Special Events and Logistics (SEaL)

The Special Events and Logistics Department (SEaL) is the muscle of AdCore. The department takes charge of logistics by systematically controlling the flow of materials and equipment of the organization. SEaL members get things done quickly, effectively and strategically. They are the movers of the team, pushing to make sure all operations run smoothly in every AdCore event. As a testament to this, SEaLs conceptualize and spearhead the core event every semester. So when you need the fusion of strength, efficiency, and innovation, call in the SEaL.

AdCore Week

AdCore Week is a celebration of the organization to showcase and share its humble growth not only to the business administration community but to the whole university. The Week contains events such as AdCore Gives Back, the Gallery Launch, PR Event, the Grand Pakain, and the Culminight.

AdVenture

AdVenture is the organization’s exposure trip to the country’s top advertising agencies, production houses, and various institutions which contribute to the whole advertising industry. Through these trips, AdCore’s applicants and members are given a glimpse of an adperson’s workplace and lifestyle. AdVenture also opens its doors for those interested in pursuing a career in advertising.

BrandHub

BrandHub is AdCore’s corporate account team, which aims to build equity for a client’s brand through brand activation events. The format of the project is tailored to fit the client’s objectives, oftentimes to generate awareness and trial. Past BrandHub projects include Unilever’s U-Tour and Pepero Day 2014.

TeenSpeak

TeenSpeak is UP AdCore’s biggest income generating event—an annual university-wide idea generation competition, in partnership with a corporate client. The competition allows UP students to have a voice as consumers in tackling challenges posed by our partner brands. TeenSpeak boasts of having had tie-ups with some of the Philippines’ top companies and brands,such as Coca-Cola, Globe, Rexona, Greenwich and P&G.

AdHouse

AdHouse is the organization’s tambayan. The AdHouse team is primarily in-charge of maintaining the tambayan’s cleanliness and orderliness. The team also handles the storage and inventory of all equipment, and materials in the tambayan.

Under the President

Under the President houses projects not classified as belonging to the five main department functions. As the name suggests, these projects are administered by the President and mostly deal with AdCore initiatives that are not directly related with just one department function.

AdCore Reps to the BAC

The UP AdCore Representatives to the Business Administration Council (AdCore Reps to BAC) is responsible for uniting the organization in participating in BAC projects. Team Reps makes sure that AdCore is visible and relevant in its home college, and that the organization takes advantage of available opportunities in VSB to bond and unite the organization’s members. Projects in the second semester include BACBACAN and BA Week. The project is not limited to VSB students.

PANA-FAO

The team represents UP AdCore in the Philippine Association of National Advertisers Federation of Advertising Organizations. They interact with colleagues from PANA-FAO member-schools, as well as professionals in the field of advertising through PANA. Moreover, they spearhead all of AdCore’s initiatives as assigned by the FAO council.

InfoCore

Information Core (InfoCore) is the right hand of the ExeCore responsible for the collection, organization, and dissemination of all relevant internal information, making sure that the organization and its stakeholders are always up to date with what’s happening inside the org, that files are properly archived, and that members are informed of their current status and grade throughout the semester.

CONCOM

The Constitution COMELEC (CONCOM) Team is in charge of reviewing the AdCore constitution and handling the election process. Throughout the semester, CONCOM will study the constitution and find ways to improve and strengthen it. They will also be the election organizers for Year 10, handling everything from collecting nominations to announcing next year’s Executive Core members. Only graduating members are allowed to apply for CONCOM.
