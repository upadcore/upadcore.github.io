Department Members

All members of UP AdCore belong to a certain department, selected when they get accepted as members, and renewed every semester. They can serve either as a manager of that department in different teams, a project head, or an AVP.

Department Projects

UP AdCore's projects are classified into departments, depending on the project's objective and contribution to the organization.

AdSchool

AdSchool is UP AdCore's official training vessel for effective visual communication. The team shall organize on and off campus learning sessions which will allow our members and future members to develop their design sensibilities and to equip them with competitive creative skills to come up with more innovative and quality works.

AdVance

AdVance brings the advertising world closer to AdCore members by giving them a glimpse of how advertising, marketing, and other corporate agencies work in order to have a better knowledge of advertising as a career path. The team provides learning opportunities about about contemporary, effective, and responsible advertising through seminars and talks conducted by reputable advertising professionals.

APPLICATION PROCESS

Your application process is as simple as spelling ADCORE. Go through with the following steps and be a part of this creativity.

A

Attend AdCoreâ€™s events. You can attend more than the required number of events. Complete the required 30 hours of Tambay. Hang out at the AdHouse and get to know other applicants and members.

D

A buddy family (composed of 2-3 members and 3-6 applicants) will have to accomplish buddy family tasks, which will have corresponding points. Each family will have to meet the required number of points announced by Team AdDition.

C

Ask the members to sign your sigsheet, especially the ExeCore and Team AdDition. Design it as creatively as you can and enjoy getting to know the members more.

O

Offer help to the AdCore teams and be an intern! This is your chance to have hands-on experience of handling an AdCore project and learning how teams work in AdCore.

R

In advertising, the pitching of ideas is what we call a Revalida. This incorporates all the learnings you and your groupmates acquired throughout your application as you present to the ExeCore and Team AdDition.

E

This will test your knowledge about AdCore and your dedication to join the organization.


UP Advertising Core holds its application process every semester. If you are a UP Diliman student looking for an avenue to express your creativity, this might just be the organization for you! Keep posted on our Facebook page to learn more about how to apply to UP AdCore.
