What is UP AdCore?


Inspired involvement starts here

UP AdCore is the home to dynamic advertising enthusiasts from all colleges and schools of UP Diliman. Primarily composed of students from the Virata School of Business, College of Fine Arts, and College of Mass Communication, we are your pitch-perfect team of portfolio-backed artists and marketers.

PHILIPPINE STUDENT QUILL AWARDS


Organized by the IABC, the PSQA honors excellence in communication in the undergraduate levels. UP AdCore was one of the organizations to have received two Awards of Excellence, the higher tier award in PSQA's recognition hierarchy.
FAO AWARDS



UP Advertising Core was hailed as the Organization of the Year for the first-ever Federation of Advertising Organizations Awards this 2013, participated on by different organizations from schools all over NCR.
AIESEC YOUTH LEADERSHIP AWARDS


The AIESEC Youth Leadership Awards aims to celebrate five youth organizations who, driven by a clear purpose, have shown exemplary leadership through leading by example and by continuous improvement in their fields. UP AdCore is one of the five recipients of this pioneer award by AIESEC Philippines.
SMART LEAGUE OF LEADERS


The SMART League of Leaders is Smart Prepaid's advocacy project that seeks to empower student groups with the skills, knowledge and resources they need to reach their goals. UP AdCore was hailed as one of the top 10 organizations and an official part of the SMART League of Leaders last April 11.
VILLAR SIPAG LEAGUE OF EXCELLENCE AWARDS


The Villar Sipag League of Excellence is an awards night organized by the UP Business League to recognize the most notable feats accomplished by its 16 member organizations. Various categories are contended for and top performers are awarded. In the past 2 years, Up AdCore has clinched 7 of these prestigious awards, 3 of them belonging to the Platinum Award Category; including the most notable: Organization of the Year.
MOST WELL-ATTENDED ACLE


USC has awarded various organizations who were able to organize outstanding learning experiences for the students. UP AdCore received the most well-attended ACLE having been able to fill the Benitez Hall with students who were interested learn more about stories behind well-known commercials.
